Meal box Story

Ingredients

We use SGS-certified ingredients for our meals. We offer a wide variety of meal box options that are low in oil and calories. Our meal boxes are made from eco-friendly materials, ensuring sustainability. We prepare our meal boxes daily to ensure freshness of the ingredients.

Utensils

We aim to reduce carbon emissions by using eco-friendly utensils. Having the restaurant personally deliver the meals adds a personal touch and enhances the overall experience. Disposable utensils typically refer to plastic knives, spoons, disposable bamboo chopsticks, paper plates, and cups, which are inexpensive and can be discarded after use.

Environmental Sustainability

While disposable utensils can help prevent the transmission of saliva-borne diseases and provide convenience for eating on-the-go, they contribute to waste management and environmental issues. The disposal of these utensils creates a burden on waste management systems, and their production also contributes to carbon emissions.
